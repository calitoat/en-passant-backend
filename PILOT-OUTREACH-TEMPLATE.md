# TrustBridge Pilot Outreach Templates

## Strategy: "Free Integration, Revenue Share"

Instead of selling badges directly to end-users, offer **free integration** to platforms with a revenue share model. This is the "Trojan Horse" approach.

**Value Prop:** "We eliminate fake leads/applications for free. You only pay when it works."

## Objection Handling

**"We already have verification"**
> Most verification is easily bypassed (phone numbers, email, etc.). TrustBridge uses OAuth with established accounts (Gmail, LinkedIn) that take years to build. Much harder to fake.

**"Our users won't want to connect their accounts"**
> We've seen 60%+ opt-in rates when positioned as "get verified to stand out." Users WANT to prove they're real when it gives them an advantage.

**"How is this different from Persona/Jumio/ID verification?"**
> Those require government ID and are expensive ($2-5 per verification). TrustBridge is lightweight OAuth - 30 seconds, no ID upload, much cheaper.

**"What about privacy?"**
> We only access public profile info and email verification status. No email content, no connections list, no private data. Users can revoke access anytime.

**"We're too small/early for this"**
> Perfect timing! Bake trust into your platform from day one. It's a competitive moat that's hard to add later.

## Quick Research Checklist

Before reaching out:
- [ ] What's their user base size?
- [ ] Do they have existing verification? What kind?
- [ ] Who's the right contact? (Product, Growth, Founder)
- [ ] What's their business model? (How do they make money?)
- [ ] Recent news/funding? (Good conversation starter)
- [ ] Competitors with verification? (Creates urgency)
